# Fight Simulator: "Styles make Fights"

___The idea of this simulator is the replicate the idea that "Styles make fights".
Certain styles of fighting have certain advantages and disadvantages against some other styles.___


1. The implementation uses a randomn generator constrained by the class attribute boxing Style ["infighter", "outboxer"] for defense.

2. The attacks are weighted by the kind of attack.

3. The damage is weighted by the relationship of attack and defense, which is safed as a dictionary.
